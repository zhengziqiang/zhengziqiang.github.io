Short Bio

I am currently a first-year Phd student at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology this incoming September, supervised by Prof. Sai-Kit Yeung. I received my bachelor degree from Ocean University of China (OUC) in 2019. After graduation, I joined UISEE. By the end of 2020, I joined in CFM center of University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) as a research assistant. My research interests lie in computer vision and deep learning.
